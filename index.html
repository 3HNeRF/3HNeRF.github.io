Abstract

In this paper, we propose a novel framework named 3H-NeRF that can generate high-resolution audio-driven talking portrait videos with high definition and high fidelity. Specifically, our framework includes talking head generation and super-resolution with torso processing modules. Firstly, we propose an efficient grid-based NeRF to generate a high-fidelity low-resolution talking head, by using the encoded volume density estimation and audio-eye-aware color calculation. This module can capture natural eye blinks and high-frequency details, and maintain almost the same training time as previous grid-based methods. Secondly, different from previous works training a NeRF network for the torso, we present a new idea to process the torso by adopting a super-resolution module on the static scene to directly generate the high-resolution portrait with our low-resolution head. Surprisingly, this simple strategy can generate a high-definition head with natural torso movements. Extensive experiments demonstrate that our method can generate more distinct and realistic talking videos with high resolution (900 × 900) compared to state-of-the-art methods, while only requiring low memory for training.
